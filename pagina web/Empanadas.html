How to make Empanadas

Ingredients:
10 ripe Bananas
Half a cup of sugar
2 cinnamon sticks
1 teaspoon vanilla
Half a cup of milk
4 tablespoons cornstarch
2 teaspoons of sugar
Oil
Preparation
  1. Cut each banana into four parts. Five cups of water are poured into a deep bowl along with the sugar, vanilla and cinnamon.
  2. Let it cook over medium heat for about half an hour or until the bananas are cooked.
  3. When this happens, let them cool. Then, in another container, add the milk and cornstarch little by little until it is well diluted. A little sugar is added.
  4. Always checking so that it is not too sweet. Add a teaspoon of vanilla and cinnamon powder. Then it is cooked over medium heat, always stirring until it thickens, begins to boil and is allowed to cool.
  5. Mash the bananas with a fork until a dough remains. A little of the mala is taken and small tortillas are formed, these tortillas are going to be put with the cold and hard milk, the tortilla is closed like an envelope, sealing the edges so that the filling does not come out.
  6. Then continue to fry each empanada in hot oil until the empanadas are golden brown.
  7. Ready to enjoy with a little sugar sprinkled on top.
